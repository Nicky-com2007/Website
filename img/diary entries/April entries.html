As I said in my March entry my mum is trying to cook new things and after lunch today she made some muffins which were delicious. I will include the recipe under.

  • 300 grams of flour
  • 150 grams of brown sugar
  • 75 grams of bitter cocoa
  • 1 cup of seed oil
  • 1 cup of almond milk
  • 200 grams of pistachios
  • 1 sachet of organic yeast

Method

  • Sift the flour and then pour all the ingridients except the pistachios and yeast.
  • Make sure you get a very soft dough to which you can add the crumbled pistachios and the baking powder.
  • Place the mixture obtained in the molds and then bake at 180 degrees for about 15 minutes.
